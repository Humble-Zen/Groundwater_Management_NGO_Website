About Jalveer Gatha

Empowering Communities through Sustainable Water Management

Our Mission

To promote sustainable groundwater management practices and empower communities to become stewards of their water resources through education, technology, and community engagement.

Our Journey

2020 - Foundation

Established Jalveer Gatha with a focus on groundwater conservation and community education.

2021 - First Initiative

Launched our first water monitoring program across 50 communities.

2022 - Educational Outreach

Developed comprehensive educational programs and interactive learning tools.

2023 - Technology Integration

Implemented advanced monitoring systems and data analysis tools.
